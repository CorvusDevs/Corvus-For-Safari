Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026 · Last Updated: April 6, 2026

CorvusDevs ("we", "us", "our") develops and publishes software applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in Corvus RSS Reader for Safari.

Our Commitment

Corvus RSS Reader is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Corvus RSS Reader does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry — We do not track how you use the extension
  • No personal information — We do not collect names, emails, IP addresses, or device identifiers
  • No third-party tracking — We do not embed advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking pixels
  • Local storage only — Your feeds, articles, folders, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Safari's extension storage and are never sent to any server

Network Requests

Corvus RSS Reader makes the following network requests to function:

  • RSS/Atom feed fetching — Corvus RSS Reader fetches feed URLs that you subscribe to in order to retrieve new articles. These requests go directly from your browser to the feed publisher's server.
  • Favicon loading — Corvus RSS Reader loads site favicons via DuckDuckGo's icon service (icons.duckduckgo.com) to display feed icons. No personal data is sent.
  • YouTube channel resolution — When subscribing to a YouTube channel, Corvus RSS Reader may use YouTube's InnerTube API to resolve channel IDs. No authentication or personal data is involved.

All requests are made directly from your browser. We do not proxy, log, or intercept any network traffic.

Website

This website (hosted via GitHub Pages) does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts.

Children's Privacy

Corvus RSS Reader is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date.
